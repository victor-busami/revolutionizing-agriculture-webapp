Dataset Information
Column Descriptions
Farm_ID
Unique identifier for each farm
Crop_Type
Type of crop grown (e.g., Rice, Wheat, Maize)
Farm_Area(acres)
Size of the farm in acres
Irrigation_Type
Method of irrigation used
Fertilizer_Used(tons)
Amount of fertilizer used in tons
Pesticide_Used(kg)
Amount of pesticide used in kilograms
Yield(tons)
Crop yield in tons (target variable)
Soil_Type
Type of soil in the farm
Season
Growing season (e.g., Rabi, Kharif, Zaid)
Water_Usage(cubic meters)
Amount of water used for irrigation
Seasons Explanation
Monsoon (Kharif)
Crops sown in the rainy season (July-October)
Winter (Rabi)
Crops sown in winter season (October-March)
Summer (Zaid)
Crops grown during the short summer season
Soil Types Overview
Loamy
Balanced mix of sand, silt and clay - generally fertile
Sandy
Quick-draining soil with poor nutrient retention
Clay
Heavy, dense soil with good nutrient retention but poor drainage
Silty
Smooth soil with good water retention and nutrients
Peaty
High organic content soil, typically acidic
